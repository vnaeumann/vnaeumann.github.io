#RULES.

1. be on time. even early if required.
2. be respectful.
3. be bold and brave.
4. before doing something ask yourself if it is the right thing to do.
5. smell good.
6. look presentable.
7. maintain eye contact.
8. start work early. finish on time.
9. if it takes less than 5 minutes, do it immediately.
10. sleep is the most important activity of the day. don't compromise on it.
11. Don't take shortcuts where it isn't required.
12. be respectful of people's choices, taste and decision.
13. Work hard. Most things are normally simple. Just work hard and finish it.
14. you are in charge of your identity. you become what you think. you become what you let others think of you.
